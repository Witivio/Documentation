  </p>

## ✨ What's improved

<div class="intercom-container"><img src="/assets/img/teams-pro/calendar-pro/calendar-pro-15.jpg"></div><p class="no-margin"></p>

### 🔹 Cleaner navigation with centralized filters

We've simplified the interface by grouping all filters into a single place.

**How to access it:**
- Click on the **filter icon** in the navigation bar

**You'll find:**
- Tags  
- Synchronized calendars (if any)  
- Time zone settings  

👉 Everything is now centralized for a smoother and more efficient experience.

---

### 🔍 New search experience

Finding your events is now faster than ever.

**How it works:**
- Click on the **search (🔍) icon**  
- The search bar opens  
- Type the **name of your event** to launch the search  

👉 Instantly access the events you're looking for without manual navigation.

---

### 📅 New Agenda view

We've introduced a new way to visualize your schedule with the **Agenda view**.

**How to access it:**
- Go to the **view selector button**  
- Choose between:  
  - Day  
  - Week  
  - Month  
  - **Agenda (new)**  

**Why it matters:**
- Clear, chronological display of your events  
- Easier to scan and follow your schedule  
- Better focus on your priorities  

👉 A more structured way to manage your time.
