# Manager Insight Pro - Get Started Guide 

Manager Insight Pro is an AI-powered management assistant available directly inside Microsoft Copilot and Microsoft Teams.
It helps managers get visibility on their team, projects, and key metrics through natural, intelligent conversations — without leaving their workspace.
Manager Insight Pro provides read-only insights based on your permissions. It never modifies data.
Manager Insight Pro may require activation or admin consent depending on your organization's setup. Once approved, it becomes available for authorized users.

---
## Prerequisites

- Active **Microsoft 365 Copilot** license (required)
- Access to **Microsoft Teams**, **Outlook**, and **Calendar**
- Admin deployment via the Microsoft 365 Admin Center (for organization-wide rollout)

## Installation & Access (via Microsoft Teams or Copilot)

Manager Insight Pro is available inside both **Microsoft Copilot** and **Microsoft Teams**.

How to access Manager Insight Pro
1. Open Microsoft Copilot
2. Go to Copilot Agents
3. Select Manager Insight Pro
4. Start asking your questions
Or via Microsoft Teams:
1. Open Microsoft Teams
2. Go to Apps
3. Search for Manager Insight Pro
4. Open the application with copilot
If Manager Insight Pro does not appear, your administrator may need to enable it in the Teams Admin Center.


## Key Features

### Chat with Your Data

Ask questions about how you manage your team and get instant, structured insights. See how your time and attention are distributed across your direct reports, identify risks early, and receive clear actions to strengthen your leadership.

### Copilot-Native Experience

Fully integrated into Microsoft 365 Copilot. No new interfaces. No complex setup. Just instant leadership insights through natural conversation.

### Reveal Real Engagement Patterns

Measure collaboration using emails, meetings, and Teams interactions. Understand not just how often you interact with each direct report, but what the content and tone of those interactions reveal about your leadership dynamics.

### Data Sources

Manager Insights Pro analyzes signals from:

- **Microsoft Teams** — channel conversations, chats, personal calls
- **Outlook** — emails sent and received
- **Calendar** — meeting frequency, duration, participants
- **Org Chart** — direct report structure

> All data stays within your Microsoft 365 tenant. Nothing is sent to external servers.

### What Manager Insights Pro Does Not Do

- It does **not** access other users' mailboxes or data
- It does **not** send, delete, or modify any emails or messages
- It does **not** take any action on your behalf
- It does **not** share your data outside your tenant
