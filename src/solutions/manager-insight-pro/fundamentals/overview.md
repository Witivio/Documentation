# Fundamentals 

Manager Insight Pro is built around natural-language interactions.
Instead of manually checking multiple tools or reports, you simply ask questions, and Manager Insight Pro aggregates and summarizes information relevant to your role as a manager.
Manager Insight Pro provides built-in suggestions to help you explore team activity, workload, and key metrics, and supports freeform prompting for deeper analysis.

---

## Prompt Suggestions (built-in)

Manager Insight Pro includes suggestion prompts designed to guide managers through common tasks.

##### What's my team's activity this week?
Manager Insight Pro analyzes recent activity and highlights:
- Key updates
- Pending items
- Collaboration patterns

##### Summarize project status
Manager Insight Pro reviews your projects and provides:
- Status overview
- Blockers or risks
- Next steps

##### Give me a quick digest of my team
Manager Insight Pro generates a compact overview including:
- Main topics and priorities
- Recent activity
- Suggested focus areas

You can also ask freeform questions to get custom insights based on your permissions and data.
