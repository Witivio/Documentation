# Q&A 

### Is activation always required?
Depending on your organization, admin consent or activation may be required for Manager Insight Pro to access management data.

### Why do I see an activation message?
Because admin consent has not yet been performed for Manager Insight Pro.

### What should I do?
Send the activation link from the card to your admin, or contact your IT administrator.

### Who should receive the link?
CEO, CTO, IT Manager, Office Manager, or Microsoft 365 administrator — depending on who manages apps.

### Does Manager Insight Pro access every user's data?
No. It only accesses data you are authorized to see, based on your role and permissions.

### Can Manager Insight Pro modify data?
No. It is designed to provide read-only insights.
