# Q&A 

### Does Manager Insights Pro require a Microsoft 365 Copilot license?
Yes. Manager Insights Pro is a Declarative Agent for Microsoft 365 Copilot and requires an active Copilot license to function.

### What data does Manager Insights Pro analyze?
It analyzes metadata from your Teams conversations, Outlook emails, Calendar meetings, and Org Chart structure. It looks at frequency, participants, timing, and interaction patterns — not the full content of every message.

### Who should receive the link?
CEO, CTO, IT Manager, Office Manager, or Microsoft 365 administrator — depending on who manages apps.

### Does it read the content of my messages?
Manager Insights Pro uses Microsoft 365 Copilot's access to your data. It processes interaction signals and content to generate summaries and insights, always within the boundaries of your own account and tenant.

### Can my manager or HR see my Manager Insights Pro results?
No. Each manager accesses only their own insights, based on their own interactions with their direct reports. There is no organizational dashboard or reporting layer for HR or leadership by default.

### Does it take any actions on my behalf?**
No. Manager Insights Pro is a read-and-analyze agent only. It never sends messages, modifies calendar events, or takes any action in your environment.
