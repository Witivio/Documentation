# Q&A 

 

### Why don’t I see GPT Pro in Teams? 

Your admin may need to allow it in the <a href="https://admin.teams.microsoft.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Teams Admin Center</a>   



 

### Why can’t I use GPT Pro after installing it? 

A license must be **assigned by an administrator** before the app becomes active. 

 

### Does GPT Pro access Teams chats or emails? 

No. GPT Pro only processes the text you manually type. 

 

### Does GPT Pro perform actions? 

No. GPT Pro only generates text suggestions. 

 

### What happens when I reach my message limit? 

GPT Pro pauses until the rolling window refreshes or your plan is upgraded. 

 

### Is GPT Pro GDPR compliant? 

Yes — fully hosted on Azure in Europe.
