## What's New in This Update

Welcome to the latest version! We're excited to introduce some fantastic improvements:

### Bug Fixes:

1. **Pagination Display:** Fixed the issue causing pagination to appear even when there were less elements to display.
2. **History Content:** Resolved the issue causing history content to display as raw, unformatted markdown.
3. **Task Module Messages:** Fixed the bug that caused messages sent through a task module to not be counted as user messages.
4. **Profile Images:** Fixed the incorrect display of user profile images.
5. **YouTube Link:** Resolved the issue causing the YouTube link to be cloned when translating a welcome card.
6. **Pop-up Reset:** Fixed the bug that caused the choice to be reset but still displayed when closing the pop-up.
7. **Consent Error:** Fixed the 500 error that occurred when logging in from a new tenant and not consenting the app.
8. **Prompt Category:** Resolved the issue causing the category to be displayed/selected for the menu when creating a prompt and selecting shared.

### New Features:

1. **GPT Pro Chatbot:** Communicate with a GPT Pro Chatbot in a chat interface on Microsoft Teams.
2. **Conversation Reset:** Start a new conversation with GPT Pro as an administrator to reset the context of the conversation.
3. **Usage KPI Tracking:** Track usage KPIs on your GPT Pro to have a better understanding of your collaborators' usage.
4. **Plugin Management:** Add and remove plugins for your users to add additional features depending on your needs.
5. **User Management:** Add users into the app to manage plugins and access to GPT Pro.
6. **Automatic Deployment:** Deploy updates automatically as a developer.
7. **Sign In/Sign Up:** Sign in or sign up as a new customer.
8. **Admin Site Navigation:** Navigate in the admin site as a customer.
9. **Usage Data Access:** Access usage data as a customer.
10. **Bot Manifest Download:** Set up a bot to download the manifest as a customer.
11. **Subscription Details:** View subscription details as a customer.
12. **Document Management:** Add and remove documents for your users to handle their status and modify the list of available documents as an administrator.
13. **Prompt and Welcome Card Management:** Manage and create prompts and welcome cards to provide a better user experience while chatting with the bot as an administrator.
14. **Administrator Management:** Manage the administrators of your license to add/delete other administrators as an administrator.
15. **System Prompt Customization:** Customize the way your chatbot will answer as an administrator.
16. **Document Indexing:** Upload and index documents so the chatbot can answer questions about them as an administrator.
17. **Document Questioning:** Ask questions to the chatbot about documents and get the proper answer as a user.
18. **User Conversation History Access:** Access the history of the user's conversation as an administrator.
19. **Prompt View and Add:** View and add prompts as an administrator.
20. **Plugin Subscription:** Subscribe to plugins within the Teams App as a user.
21. **Prompt Sharing:** Share a prompt within your company as a user.
22. **AI Statistics:** Have AI statistics about the questions asked by the users as an administrator.
23. **User History Review:** Review your users' history to assess the quality of the answers as an administrator.
