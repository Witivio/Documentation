# Preview Features – AI Desk Pro
 
## What are Preview features and how do I enable them?
 
---
 
## What are Preview features?
 
Preview features in AI Desk Pro provide early access to selected capabilities before their broader availability.
 
They allow customers to:
 
- Discover upcoming enhancements  
- Evaluate new behaviors or configurations  
- Provide structured feedback  
- Anticipate future platform evolution  
 
Preview features:
 
- Are governed by specific **Supplemental Preview Terms of Use**  
- Are intended for controlled evaluation  
- May evolve during the Preview phase  
- May have limited scope or availability  
- May be updated, refined, or withdrawn at any time  
 
Enabling a Preview feature does not constitute a roadmap commitment or a guarantee of general availability.
 
---
 
## Important considerations before enabling Preview
 
Before activating Preview mode:
 
- Ensure activation is limited to appropriate agents  
- Avoid relying on Preview features for critical business processes  
- Inform relevant internal stakeholders  
- Review the applicable Preview Terms  
 
Preview features are designed for controlled evaluation environments.
 
---
 
## How do I enable Preview mode?
 
### Prerequisites
 
To enable Preview mode, you must:
 
- Have administrator permissions for the relevant agent  
- Have access to the agent configuration settings  
 
If you do not have the required permissions, contact your system administrator.
 
---
 
### Enable Preview mode (Agent-level activation)
 
1. Sign in to AI Desk Pro  
2. Open the relevant Agent  
3. Navigate to **Agent Personalization**  
4. Locate the **Preview Mode** setting  
5. Toggle Preview Mode to **On**  
6. Save your changes  
 
Preview mode becomes active immediately after saving.
 
---
 
## What happens when Preview mode is activated?
 
Once enabled:
 
- The selected Preview capabilities become active on the agent  
- The agent’s behavior or internal logic may evolve during the Preview phase  
- Changes may occur without additional notice  
 
Preview mode can be disabled at any time by toggling the setting to **Off**.
