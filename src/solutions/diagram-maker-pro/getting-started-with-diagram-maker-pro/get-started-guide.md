# Diagram Maker Pro - Get Started Guide

Diagram Maker Pro is an AI-powered Copilot Declarative Agent available directly inside Microsoft 365 Copilot.
It helps professionals turn ideas into clear, structured visual diagrams using natural language — without switching tools or learning any diagram syntax.
Diagram Maker Pro generates Mermaid diagrams instantly from your descriptions. It never modifies your data.
Diagram Maker Pro may require activation or admin consent depending on your organization's setup. Once approved, it becomes available for authorized users.

---

## Prerequisites

- Active **Microsoft 365 Copilot** license (required)
- Access to **Microsoft Teams** or **Microsoft 365 Copilot**
- Admin deployment via the Microsoft 365 Admin Center (for organization-wide rollout)

## Installation & Access

Diagram Maker Pro is available inside **Microsoft 365 Copilot**.

How to access Diagram Maker Pro:
1. Open **Microsoft Copilot**
2. Go to **Copilot Agents**
3. Select **Diagram Maker Pro**
4. Start describing your diagram

If Diagram Maker Pro does not appear, your administrator may need to enable it in the Teams Admin Center.

## Key Features

### Natural Language to Diagram

Describe what you need in plain language — Diagram Maker Pro analyzes the structure and generates the most appropriate diagram automatically. No syntax, no drawing required.

### Copilot-Native Experience

Fully integrated into Microsoft 365 Copilot. No new interfaces. No complex setup. Just instant professional diagrams through natural conversation.

### Supported Diagram Types

Diagram Maker Pro generates the following diagram types:

- **Flowcharts** — step-by-step processes and decision trees
- **Mind maps** — idea structures and concept hierarchies
- **Gantt charts** — project timelines and task dependencies
- **Journey diagrams** — user or customer experience maps
- **Quadrant charts** — prioritization and positioning matrices
- **Pie charts** — proportional breakdowns
- **Requirement diagrams** — functional and technical specifications

### Powered by Mermaid

All diagrams are generated as **Mermaid diagrams** — a standard, widely supported format ready for use in documents, wikis, SharePoint, and presentations.

### What Diagram Maker Pro Does Not Do

- It does **not** access or modify your files, emails, or calendar
- It does **not** send or publish diagrams on your behalf
- It does **not** share your data outside your tenant
