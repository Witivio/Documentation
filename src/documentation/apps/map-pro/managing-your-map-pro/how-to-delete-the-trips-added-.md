# How to delete the Trips added?

<p class="no-margin">You can delete the trips added under the <b>Trips</b> tab by clicking on the delete icon and confirm it.</p>
<p class="no-margin"></p>
<ol>
<li>
<p class="no-margin">Select <b>Delete</b> icon.</p>
